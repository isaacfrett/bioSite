Carolyn Lo

Carolyn is a person that always wants to make herself and those who are her better in what they do. She’s uplifting in the fact that her purpose is to help those that need help whether that be with their health, physical disability, or emotional support, she always seems to give willingly where it’s needed most. She credit’s this drive to her upbringing and how she was raised. Her parents, who came from Vietnam before she was born, raised her with great values and instilled in her a hard work ethic that would later help her in college. Being a first generation college student, she pushed herself to venture into a big University where she didn’t know anyone and was away from her family. At college, she double majored in Biochemistry and Human Physiology and graduated with honors all while holding leadership roles in her Vietnamese sorority. One of the bigger moments in her life came after college when she started to work in the field of DNA manufacturing. She gained a different view on the world when she realized the importance of personal finance and what her family had provided for her during her upbringing.

For the future, Carolyn wants to go to graduate school to become a Physician’s Assistant and spend her time working with patients face to face. She has goals of traveling the world and exploring new places that she hasn’t been before. She hopes that there’s a possibility that as she travels she can administer to people all around the world that need help with their health. She’s persistent about changing the world, and she’s motivated to make an impact every morning she wakes up. Her big heart and her compassionate spirit is going to better the lives of so many people in a positive way.
