Healthcare

Carolyn's biggest motivation in life is to work in the healthcare field and specifically in patient care. She's always been the type of person that enjoys helping people face to face and with conditions that are hard for them to overcome on their own. She's worked as a Nursing Assistant in the past and she now has her eyes set on going to Physician’s Assistant school to work in medicine upon graduating. She's passionate about helping others and this is one of the many ways she hopes to be able to help others in the future.

Education

Carolyn's accomplishments include being a first generation college student in her family and graduating with two degrees in both Biochemistry and Human Physiology. She did this while holding various leadership positions in a Vietnamese sorority. She currently uses her degrees to work in the DNA manufacturing industry but as mentioned earlier, she hopes to attend Physician’s Assistant school within the year and work in medicine there after.

Fitness and Health

From working in the health field and from her own goals and aspirations, Carolyn is very interested in the fitness field and actively likes to workout, lift weights, participate in physical events like snowboarding and ax throwing. She values physical activity and working out as essential to her overall health and well-being. She knows that her motivation and drive wouldn't be the same if she didn't actively push herself in all areas of her life.
